## Culture Key Lens

The Culture Key Pre-Scale Risk Lens examines three structural questions
before an AI system scales:

1. Decision Structure — Who holds authority and veto power?
2. Control Capacity — How fast can escalation and oversight occur?
3. System Recovery — Can the system safely rollback and contain failures?

If these structures are unclear, scale amplifies risk.

Governance capacity becomes the real bottleneck at scale.
