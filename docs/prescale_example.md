# 📘 Culture Key — Example Prescale Application

This example illustrates how Culture Key governance checks can be applied before an AI system scales into real-world deployment.

The purpose is to demonstrate how structural risks are identified and addressed **prior to scale**.

---

## Scenario

A conversational AI support system is preparing to scale from pilot deployment to over 1M active users.

The system has demonstrated strong performance metrics, but the organization wants to evaluate **governance readiness before scaling further**.

---

## Detected Structural Risk

During the Culture Key prescale review, interaction logs and escalation policies were examined.

- ambiguous user intent detection  
- potential emotional manipulation during high-stress support interactions  
- unclear escalation triggers for sensitive cases

This combination created a **decision integrity risk** during high-volume interactions.

---

## Culture Key Governance Check Triggered

Ethical State Machine → **REVIEW state**

The system was flagged for additional governance review before scaling approval.

---

## Governance Decision

The Culture Key governance process required:

- escalation path clarification  
- human oversight expansion for sensitive cases  
- response policy adjustments for emotional inference scenarios

These changes were documented through the Ethical Decision Log process.

---

## Mitigation Actions

The organization implemented:

- revised escalation thresholds  
- additional human-in-the-loop review triggers  
- safeguards against emotional manipulation patterns  
- updated monitoring signals for high-risk interactions

---

## Outcome

The system was cleared for a **controlled scaling phase** with monitoring enabled.

Governance logs were recorded to ensure **decision traceability and future auditability**.

---

## Why This Matters

Culture Key helps organizations identify governance weaknesses **before they become system failures at scale**.

The framework focuses on structural readiness rather than model performance alone.

> Governance capacity becomes the real bottleneck at scale.
