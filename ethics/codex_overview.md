The Culture Key Codex is the ethical backbone of the Culture Key ecosystem.

Its purpose is to ensure that every AI interaction remains:

- safe  
- transparent  
- respectful  
- consent-based  
- non-manipulative  

The Codex defines the **governing principles**, not the implementation details.

It provides the public ethical architecture, while technical rules,
decision logic, state transitions, and enforcement mechanisms remain
within the protected private environment.

---

## What the Codex Ensures

- AI behaviour follows defined ethical constraints  
- human authority remains final  
- safety takes precedence over efficiency  
- clarity takes precedence over ambiguity  
- high-risk scenarios remain reviewable and traceable  

---

The Codex is a living governance framework that evolves under
explicit human oversight.
