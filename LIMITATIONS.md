# ⚠️ Culture Key — Limitations (Public)

Culture Key is a governance framework designed to support responsible AI deployment.

It is important to clarify what the framework **does not attempt to do**.

---

## Not a Replacement for Organizational Responsibility

Culture Key does not replace human leadership, legal oversight, or institutional governance.

Organizations remain fully responsible for their AI systems.

---

## Not a Complete Technical Security System

Culture Key focuses on **governance structure and ethical oversight**.

It does not replace:

- cybersecurity infrastructure  
- infrastructure monitoring systems  
- model safety engineering practices  

---

## Not a Fully Autonomous Governance System

Human authority remains essential.

The framework intentionally avoids fully automated ethical decision-making.

---

## Not a Universal Solution

AI systems differ widely across domains.

Culture Key provides a **governance lens**, not a one-size-fits-all control system.

Organizations must adapt the framework to their context.

---

## Public Repository Scope

This repository contains **architecture and governance concepts only**.

It does not include:

- production infrastructure  
- operational deployments  
- internal safety tooling  

---

## Philosophy

Culture Key recognizes that **AI governance is an evolving field**.

The framework is designed to support responsible experimentation, not rigid control.
